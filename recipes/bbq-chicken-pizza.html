BBQ Chicken Pizza

Description

A bbq chicken pizza recipe featuring spicy barbecue sauce, diced chicken, peppers, onions, and cilantro.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1 cup spicy barbecue sauce
  • 2 skinless boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoncini peppers
  • 1 cup chopped red onion
  • Â½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cups shredded Colby-Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place pizza crust on a baking sheet. Spread barbecue sauce on crust. Top with chicken, pepperoncini peppers, onion, and cilantro. Cover with Colby-Jack cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 15 minutes.
